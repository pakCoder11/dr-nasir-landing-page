Understanding Mental Wellness

What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is more than just stress. It's a persistent feeling of worry or fear that can interfere with daily life. It often manifests physically as rapid heartbeat, sweating, or fatigue.

What is Depression?

Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest. It affects how you feel, think, and handle daily activities, such as sleeping, eating, or working.

Why They Occur?

Mental health conditions can stem from a mix of genetic, biological, environmental, and psychological factors. Stressful life events or trauma often act as triggers.

10 Steps to Wellness

01

Daily Routine

Establish a consistent sleep and wake schedule to regulate your body clock.

02

Mindful Breathing

Practice 4-7-8 breathing techniques to instantly calm the nervous system.

03

Limit Caffeine

Reduce stimulants that can trigger anxiety and jitters.

Take 1 hour off screens before bed to improve sleep quality.

Write down 3 things you are grateful for every morning.

06

Engage in 20 minutes of light exercise like walking or yoga.

08
